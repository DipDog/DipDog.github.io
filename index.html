The Story of DIPDOG

The DipDog was born on the Binance Smart Chain with the purpose of reaching the Moon with its token holders. However, the Founders had other plans and abandoned DipDog in the shadow of other rising Dog tokens. The community found the poor dog and decided to nurture it back to health and help it reach its new goal. To get revenge on the Founders and the Sellers who left him at the bottom.
