Static Rewards

Static Rewards solves a whole range of problems. First, the volume of trading sets the baseline conditions for the rewards. This mechanism is put in place to alleviate some of the downward sell pressure put on the token by early adapters selling their tokens after crazy high APY farming. Second, the reflect mechanism encourages holders to hold on to their bag for longer, as their bag will grow in tune with the total tokens held by the owner.

Manual Burn

SALSA aims to implement a burn strategy that is beneficial and rewarding for those engaged for the long term. Having burns controlled by the team and promoted based on achievements helps to keep the community rewarded and informed. The conditions of the manual burn and the amounts will be advertised and tracked.Furthermore, the total number of SALSA burned is featured on our website, allowing for easy tracking and transparency

Automatic LP

The automatic LP is the secret ingredient in our SALSA.

Its a two way function that works like this; The contract sucks up tokens from buyers and sellers alike, and adds them to the LP, creating a solid price floor The Taxation penalty secures some of the volume of SALSA to the LP as a reward for its holders. This creates a solid support for the price floor, and aims to cushion holders from sell offs. The goal here is to prevent the larger dips when whales decide to sell their tokens later in the game, which keeps the price from fluctuating as much as if there was no automatic LP function. All of this is an effort to alleviate some of the troubles we have seen with the Current DeFi reflection tokens.
